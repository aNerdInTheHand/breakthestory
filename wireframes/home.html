How to play

Welcome to Break The Story. You play as a football journalist trying to make your name as somebody in the know.

You will be approached by people claiming to have insider knowledge, but it is up to you whether to use them as a trusted source.

Using information your sources provide, you can write stories for broadsheet newspapers, tabloid newspapers, websites and social media.

You will gain reputation for reporting stories that come true and lose it for those that don't.

The amount of reputation at stake is affected by things like the size of the story, where it's published, and how staunchly you defended it.

To get started, enter your name and fictional social media handle to be used in the game.
